These designs were made when I was working as a full-time graphic designer at Everfriend Co., Ltd., a health food distribution start-up, in Taiwan. I have found that working in a different environment with different tastes and styles is a worthwhile experience. This has allowed me to understand the usage of colors, typography, layout, and compositions.

Most of these projects were done with limited resources. I was able to make use of these things and build something creative upon them. There were times where I had to employ an iPhone 5s to take product pictures in a portable studio. Even though there might be detail lost in the results, it still gave me an opportunity to find a way around difficult circumstances.
